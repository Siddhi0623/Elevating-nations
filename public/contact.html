01  How we partner

Different partners, one shared purpose.

From local authorities to landlords, every relationship we build is grounded in safety, dignity, and long-term stability for the people we house.

Local authorities

Councils & charities

We are building safe and supportive housing pathways for women, families, and young people requiring stable accommodation and community-focused support environments.

We work alongside referral teams to make placements simple, transparent, and centred on each person's wellbeing.

Property partners

Landlords & housing providers

We are seeking long-term property partnerships with landlords and providers interested in supporting safe, community-focused housing initiatives.

We value clean, well-maintained, ordinary homes — reliable, neutral residential spaces where people can settle and feel secure.

02  Support pathways

Support that meets people where they are.

Residents may receive:

  • Welfare check-ins
  • Housing stability support
  • Signposting to specialist services
  • Community support pathways
  • Referral guidance

Stability, dignity, affordability, and long-term independence.
